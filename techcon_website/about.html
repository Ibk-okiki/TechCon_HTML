Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for software developers and has since evolved into one of the most influential technology conferences in the world. With attendees from over 50 countries, it has become a platform for innovation, inspiration, and collaboration.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the global tech community by connecting thought leaders, fostering collaboration, and promoting cutting-edge innovation across all areas of technology. We strive to create an inclusive space where ideas flourish.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lin

A pioneer in AI ethics, Dr. Lin has led groundbreaking work in responsible machine learning. She delivered the keynote on "AI for Good" at TechCon 2021.

James Rodriguez

As the founder of CloudNova, James revolutionized edge computing. His talk in 2022 focused on decentralized cloud architecture.

Fatima Bello

Fatima is a cybersecurity expert known for her contributions to global threat prevention. She led a panel on secure systems design at TechCon 2023.
